Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a script or project proposal?

Please use the contact form above with "Film Project" selected as the inquiry type. Include a brief synopsis and your credentials.

Do you accept fan mail?

While we appreciate fan support, this portal is primarily for professional inquiries. Fan mail should be directed through official fan club channels.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please be specific about your project, timeline, and requirements. Include relevant background information and contact details.

How do I request an interview?

Select "Interview Request" in the form and provide details about your publication, audience, and proposed topics.
